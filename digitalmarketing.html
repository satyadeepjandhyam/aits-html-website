What is OFF Page SEO?

OFF Page SEO in digital marketing is a technique that helps to improve the position of your website in the search engine result pages (SERPs). To enhance the position of keywords, many people follow OFF-page SEO with link building strategies but it is not only that. In general, On-page SEO techniques concentrate on making your content visible to search engine result pages.

Following are the OFF Page SEO Techniques used in Digital Marketing:

  • Link-building
  • Social Media Management
  • Blog commenting
  • Forum Posting
  • Guest Posting
  • Directory Submissions
  • Online PR Outreach
  • Content Marketing
  • Content Optimization
